Forecasting migrant arrival numbers

This page displays forecasts for arrival numbers into countries on the Balkans route. The models are based on previous data of arrivals provided by UNHCR. Different models can be loaded via the dropdown list. The default loaded model is the one we deem most reliable. The page is split into two parts, Forecast shows estimates for the next few days and Previous Performance allows you to check how closely the model predicted previous arrival numbers.
